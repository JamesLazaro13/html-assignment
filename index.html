About Me

Hi there! I am James Lazaro, a first-year Algonquin student in the IMD program. I am a first-generation Filipino born in Ottawa, Ontario. My hobbies include photography, videography, music, rugby, camping, and working out. I also like to spend my money on bubble tea.

I am a wedding photographer outside of school and work as a server at a local restaurant in my hometown of Stittsville. I am passionate about digital art. As an individual who works with tech, taking this program will teach me tremendous amounts of information, I can incorporate into art.
